Ice Cream and Its Importance

What is Ice Cream?

Ice cream is a frozen dessert made from milk, cream, sugar, and flavoring ingredients. It is loved by people of all ages for its sweet taste and cooling effect.

Why Ice Cream is Popular

Ice cream is popular because it is delicious, refreshing, and available in many flavors. It is commonly enjoyed during hot weather and special occasions.

Ice cream brings happiness and refreshment, especially in summer.

Types of Ice Cream

  • Vanilla
  • Chocolate
  • Strawberry
  • Butterscotch
